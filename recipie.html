FOOD RESIPE

Aloo Posto

Ingredients: Potatoes (aloo), poppy seeds (posto), mustard oil, green chilies, turmeric.
Method: Sauté potatoes with poppy seed paste and spices; cook until potatoes are tender.

Avial

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables (like drumsticks, carrots, beans), coconut, yogurt, curry leaves, turmeric.
Method: Cook vegetables with coconut paste and yogurt; temper with curry leaves and mustard seeds.

Dal Baati Churma

Ingredients: Mixed lentils (dal), wheat flour, ghee, spices (cumin, coriander), sugar, almonds.
Method: Cook lentils with spices; roast wheat flour dough balls (baati); serve with ghee and churma (sweetened crumbled baati).

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers), onions, yogurt, biryani spices (bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom), saffron.
Method: Sauté onions and vegetables; layer with par-cooked rice and biryani spices; cook until rice is fluffy and vegetables are tender.
